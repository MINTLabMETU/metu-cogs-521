there the nashwan TT bars for. plug-in coupon from the Coupons forum for even more joy. just few notes the bar tops have drop midway in the bar which looks cool but does make for comfortable onthetops riding position unless you dont mind riding with your hands close together alongside the stem. the bar is standard so BMX levers not fit perhaps unless you have hinged mounts. other than that bullhorn provide lots of leverage for outofthesaddle efforts and better low-speed balance and control still learning how do trackstand. if you do go with bullhorn angle the bars upwards match the angle of your wrists when you are in your seated riding position
